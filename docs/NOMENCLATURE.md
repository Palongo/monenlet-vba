| Value | Word | S plural? |
|---|---|---|

| 1 000 | THOUSAND | Never |
| 1 000 000 | MILLION | Never |
| 1 000 000 000 | BILLION | Never |

### AND rule (British banking)

| Context | AND? | Example |
|---|---|---|
| After HUNDRED + remainder | Always | ONE HUNDRED AND ONE |
| After THOUSAND + remainder < 100 | Yes | ONE THOUSAND AND ONE |
| After THOUSAND + remainder ≥ 100 | No | ONE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED |
| After MILLION / BILLION | Never | TWO MILLION FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND |
